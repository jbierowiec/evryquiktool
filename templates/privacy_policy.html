Privacy Policy

Transparent information about how evryquiktool handles your data.

1) What We Collect

We do not collect or store any personal data on our servers.
Files you use with our tools are handled locally within your session (e.g., in your browser or temporary app memory). We do not create accounts, and we do not retain identifiers.

2) How Your Data Is Used

Any files or inputs are used only to perform the requested operation (e.g., combine a PDF, convert an image). Processing is scoped to your current session and is not persisted by us.

3) Local Session Storage & Deletion

Data (uploads/downloads, intermediate results) exists only locally per session. You can delete these items at any time from your device or session. When your session ends or you clear your browser/app data, the associated items are removed.

4) Sharing & Disclosure

We do not share your files or information with anyone. There is no selling, renting, or trading of data.

5) Cookies & Analytics

We do not use cookies or analytics tools. No tracking technologies are employed on this site.

6) Third-Party Links

We do not include links to third-party websites and do not integrate external services that would collect your data.

7) Data Security

Because processing is local to your session, you maintain control. For additional safety, you can clear your browser data or remove downloaded files. Avoid uploading sensitive content on shared devices.

8) Audience

evryquiktool is intended for all ages. There are no age-based restrictions on use.

9) Changes to This Policy

If we change how we operate, we’ll update this page to reflect the latest information.
